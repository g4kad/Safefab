Our Story

About Us

Originally envisioned and named as Safe FAB Safety Services Singapore under then SAFEFAB U.S.A., SAFEFAB made its name supplying Safety Services in Singapore and supplied Fire Protection Systems, Electrical and Mechanical and Spare Parts and accessories to foreign based customers in the Philippines and Papua New Guinea.

With Facilities Supplies and Services added to its portfolio, Power was added to SF3S — thus the name SAFEFAB Power.
